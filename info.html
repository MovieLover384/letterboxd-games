About Letterboxd Games

Letterboxd Games is a fan project inspired by the popular movie community Letterboxd. Our goal is to create fun and challenging games that test your knowledge of films and film fans around the world.

Fan Project

This project is made by film lovers, for film lovers. We are not affiliated with Letterboxd, but we celebrate its vibrant community.

How to Play

Choose a game from the homepage and follow the instructions. From guessing someone's favorite movie to comparing filmographies, each game is designed to challenge your knowledge.

Future Plans

We are constantly working on new games and features. Stay tuned for updates and new challenges to test your cinema expertise!
